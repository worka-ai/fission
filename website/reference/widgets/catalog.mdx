# Widget catalog

This is the full widget and render surface exposed through the standard `fission` setup.

Start with one category at a time and only expand once the previous layer is clear.

**Core primitives from `fission_core`**

- `Align`, `Builder`, `Button`, `ButtonContentAlign`, `ButtonVariant`, `Checkbox`,
  `Column`, `Composite`, `Container`, `Clip`, `FocusScope`, `GestureDetector`, `Grid`,
  `GridItem`, `Icon`, `Image`, `LazyColumn`, `LayoutBuilder`, `Overlay`, `Positioned`,
  `Radio`, `RichText`, `RichTextRun`, `Row`, `SafeArea`, `Scroll`, `Slider`, `Spacer`,
  `Switch`, `Text`, `TextContent`, `TextFontStyle`, `TextInput`, `TextRunStyle`, `Transform`,
  `Video`, and `ZStack`.

**Authoring composition and layout widgets from `fission_widgets`**

- `HStack`, `VStack`, `Center`, `Wrap`, `SimpleGrid`, `SplitView`, `SplitDirection`, `Card`,
  `Divider`, `Transition`, `Drawer`, `DrawerSide`, `Tabs`, `TabItem`, `Accordion`,
  `AccordionItem`, `Modal`, `ModalAction`, and `Popover`.

**Interaction and input widgets**

- `Toast`, `ToastKind`, `Tooltip`, `Select`, `SelectItem`, `Menu`, `MenuButton`, `MenuItem`,
  `Combobox`, `SegmentedControl`, `RangeSlider`, `NumberInput`, `Radio`, `Checkbox`, `Slider`,
  `Switch`, `DatePicker`, `DateRangePicker`, `TimePicker`, `FileUpload`, `Editable`, and
  `FormControl`.

**Display and product surface widgets**

- `Avatar`, `Badge`, `Tag`, `Stat`, `Calendar`, `Timeline`, `TimelineItem`, `DataTable`,
  `TableColumn`, `TableRow`, `Breadcrumb`, `BreadcrumbItem`, `Link`, `Code`, `Kbd`,
  `CircularProgress`, `ProgressBar`, `Spinner`, `Skeleton`, `Alert`, `AlertKind`, `Hero`,
  `Stepper`, `EmptyState`, `Dropzone`, `TreeView`, `TreeItem`, `Pagination`, `WebView`, and
  `DropDown`.

**Navigation and app utilities**

- `Route`, `RouteParams`, `Router`, `Portal`, `CanvasLowerer`, `canvas(width, height,
  painter)`, `flyout(anchor, content)`, and `absolute_fill(child)`.

The render node types are not widgets you build UI with directly, but they are part of
the exposed surface: `Node` and `CustomNode`.

**Desktop-only rendering helpers**

- `TerminalLaunchConfig`, `TerminalSession`, and `TerminalView` are available where the
  terminal widget is enabled.

Most teams start with `Align`, `Column`, `Row`, `Text`, `Button`, `Image`, and one stateful
input (`TextInput`, `Select`, or `Switch`) before moving across the rest of the surface.
